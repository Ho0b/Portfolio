About Me!

Growing up in a relatively strict home with my dad and aunt who were a bit too controlling changed me into the person I am today. While I don't want to blame them for the things they did to me (emotionally) as a kid, don't get me wrong they are great parents and I love them very much, parts of me still want answers. My darker half wants to know why they limited so much of my freedom, and said the things they said but I know now that their intentions were pure. They instilled so much fear and anger into my head that It made me question whether I should finish the race or give up entirely. But then I realized something, why continue to hate myself and my parents for eternity when I can just do more productive things? and so I did. At age 16 I started learning Photoshop on my own, at age 17, I learned Premiere Pro and Illustrator, at 18, I dabbed into Blender, After Effects, 21, I took classes in Web development and Graphic Design, and Now at 22 I am exploring music production. Made a couple projects since then and I have never been happier. I can safely say my greatest strength is my willingness to learn and improve, this is my drive. Although, I am able to learn the things I want. I sometimes get these depression episodes that makes me want to do nothing nor create. I have gone to therapy and have been working on these situations but sometimes I just feel like crap. Often times, It is pressure from my parents mainly my aunt but I've gotten used to it. These situations made me so caught up in my own head that it becomes hard to focus on I need to do. My emotions are my greatest weakness, and I don't think any other weakness compares. I can be filled with so much joy when working on a project one day and the next I stop everything for a week or more. But recently, I have been experimenting with using my emotions as a catalyst for my projects, and so far I've enjoyed creating a lot more.

- Netra
